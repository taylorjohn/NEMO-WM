Omnimodal Perception

Five biological senses — vision, proprioception, audio, tactile, chemical — fused into a shared 64-D belief space with attention-gated fusion. Anomalous senses get more weight.

Dual-System Cognition

Fast instinct (System 1, 7μs) for routine. Slow deliberation (System 2, 166μs) for novelty. Automatically switches based on surprise, error, and fatigue. Kahneman in silicon.

S1: 64% · S2: 36%

Grounded Language

648 words learned from experience, not text. Invents new words for novel experiences. Follows instructions ("go upper right" → reaches region_18 ✓). Grammar composed from constructions.

NeuroTransformer

5 head types: DA (surprise), ACh (explore/exploit), CRT (conflict), NE (gain), 5HT (temporal). Trained on real data: wins 3/4 vs standard transformer. 3.9% lower test MSE at 500 epochs.

What It Can't Do (Yet)

Navigate real kitchens. Speak thousands of words. Recognize faces. Manipulate objects with hands. The architecture handles all of these — the scale doesn't, yet. The claim isn't that we built a human. The claim is that we built every subsystem a human uses to think, wired them together, and proved they work.
